# StayHia Privacy & Policy Baseline

## Required Pages Before Public Launch
- Privacy Policy
- Terms of Service
- Cancellation Policy
- Refund Policy
- Cookie Notice
- Trust & Safety Standards

## Data Categories
- Account identity data
- Booking and payment metadata
- Device/session analytics
- Communication logs (host/guest support)

## Data Handling Principles
- Data minimization
- Purpose limitation
- Encryption in transit and at rest
- Access logging and least privilege
- Region-aware retention windows

## User Controls
- Download account data
- Delete account request
- Marketing consent controls
- Notification preference center

## Compliance Track
- GDPR-ready controls
- CCPA/CPRA-style disclosure controls
- Local tax + identity obligations by jurisdiction
